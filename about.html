About Us

Our Founder

Mrs. Sowunmi O. Mojisola is an extraordinary educationist whose profound dedication to nurturing children and young adults was divinely ignited. Her vision led her to establish an educational institution that has not only left an indelible mark on society but continues to shape and transform the minds of the youth. Serving as the Chief Executive Officer and Management Head of God's Pride Schools and God's Pride Educational Consultancy, Mrs. Sowunmi's leadership has been instrumental in orchestrating collaborative efforts with her team to achieve common goals, elevate standards, and create meaningful opportunities. Mrs. Sowunmi embarked on her educational journey at Ansar-Ud-Deen Comprehensive College, Ota, Ogun State, where she obtained her Senior Secondary Certificate Examination. She then pursued a National Diploma in Science Laboratory Technology at the Federal Polytechnic, Maro, graduating in 1993. Her commitment to excellence led her to complete a Higher National Diploma at the same institution in 1996. Eager to advance her expertise and further her contributions to education, Mrs. Sowunmi earned a Post Graduate Diploma in Education from Lagos State University in 2003. Her pursuit of academic excellence extended to South America University, where she obtained a Master's degree in Educational Planning and Management in 2022. Equally impressive is her attainment of a Master's degree in Innovation in Learning and Teaching from the University of South Wales, UK, in the same year. Mrs. Sowunmi's dedication to fostering holistic education is evident through her completion of the Montessori education course and active participation in seminars hosted by the Ogun State Ministry of Education Science and Technology. These seminars, such as 'Administration of Continuous Assessment within the School System' and 'Discipline Management in Schools,' have equipped her with a comprehensive skill set, enabling her to lead with insight and effectiveness. Furthermore, Mrs. Sowunmi's commitment to continuous growth is exemplified by her participation in various training programs and the acquisition of numerous certifications. Her professional development includes Child Psychology (2020), a STEM Educator Certificate Course by CODEFEST (2021), Jolly Phonics CPD College (2022), and a diploma in educational psychology from Alison (2022). Notably, she was awarded an honorary Doctor of Philosophy in Educational Psychology and Management from Prowess University Delaware, USA. Her professional journey includes serving as a Laboratory Technologist at Palmer Memorial Hospital, Ikot Usen, Akwa-Ibom State. She also worked as a Laboratory Technician with Farmes Meyer Ltd, Sango, Ogun State, from 1995 to 1996. Beyond her remarkable professional accomplishments, Mrs. Mojisola's commitment to spiritual values is evident through her active membership in the African Church Evangelical Fellowship. Her contributions to evangelism and church planting have been acknowledged and commended by the African Church, Awori Diocese Evangelical Team. Born in August 1975 in Lagos, Nigeria, Mrs. Mojisola is a cherished wife and mother, blessed with a family of responsible children. Her journey exemplifies an unwavering commitment to education, faith, and making a profound impact on the lives of individuals and communities.

The Mandate

God's Pride School is a mandate and a God-given school born out of the vision and mission to raise a generation of new academics founded on Godly values. That is why our Motto for the Primary School is "Firm Foundation" We are called to raise a child firmly rooted and established in God. Colossians 2:7.

Our Vision

Our vision is based on certain fundermental principles that are bound on basic moral, spiritual and academic excellence that will make every child to excel in different arears of life in his or her futuree endevour thus becoming a pride to his/her parent, nation the world at large and to God Almighty, the creator of heaven and earth.

Our Mission

To Create a purpose driven youth; thereby helping them identify their individual gifts, talents and potentials, thus helping them grow into educated, responsible and reliable global citizens on whom their parents, the society, the nation, the world at large and God can rely on.

Our Values

Our values are based solely on the word of God (2 Peter 1:5-7). At God's Pride School, we also teach our pupils and students practical life skills, Vocational and Entrepreneurial Skills, Life skills values, good etiquette and manners, Jolly phonics and Diction; How to speak right so that they will be able to stand out anywhere they go. Apart from academics, we help our students to develop them. The students are inspired and encouraged to work hard to achieve all-round excellence in life and also to develop their leadership qualities.

God's Pride Entrepreneurship Centre

All students irrespective of their chosen area of discipline must take two entrepreneurship courses of which computer education is compulsory.
