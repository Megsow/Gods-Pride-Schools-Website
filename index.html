Prepare for an exciting and enriching summer experience at God's Pride Group of Schools! Our Summer Coaching program promises engaging activities, learning opportunities, and fun side attractions for students of all ages.

Highlights:

  • Challenges and Competitions: Join in the thrill of friendly competitions! Test your skills in music, football, and handwriting challenges. Embrace healthy competition and showcase your talents.
  • Music Training: Unleash your musical talents with expert guidance from our skilled instructors. Explore different instruments and discover the joy of making music.
  • Robotics and Coding: Dive into the world of technology with robotics and coding workshops. Learn to build and program robots while exploring the foundations of coding.
  • Programming and Graphic Design: Unlock your creativity through programming and graphic design sessions. Develop essential digital skills and design eye-catching visuals.
  • Craft Workshops: Unleash your imagination with craft workshops. Create unique and artistic projects that showcase your creativity and craftsmanship.
  • Indoor and Outdoor Games: Stay active and have a blast with a variety of indoor and outdoor games. From sports to team-building activities, there's something for everyone.
  • Mathematics/Reading Clinic: Sharpen your math and reading skills in a supportive environment. Our experienced educators will provide guidance and practice to boost your academic abilities.

Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to make your summer unforgettable! Join us at God's Pride Group of Schools and discover a world of learning, fun, and personal growth.
